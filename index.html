About

Research at the intersection of neuroscience and computation.

I develop computational models and experimental paradigms to understand how humans represent uncertainty and learn to regulate their own brain states. My work spans fMRI, EEG, fNIRS, physiological biomarkers, and machine learning.

Before starting my PhD at UC Irvine, I completed an M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Tehran and a B.Sc. in Computer Software Engineering at Sadjad University of Technology.
